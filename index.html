Hi, I am Matthew Douglas

I am an experienced Software Developer who specializes in both web and game applications. My journey began with a love for video games and a curiosity about how they were made. While I started in game development, I’ve since evolved into a well-rounded generalist, building everything from immersive game experiences to robust web applications.

Alongside my development work, I’m also passionate about education. I teach part-time at Fanshawe College, where I lead programming courses and enjoy mentoring the next generation of developers. Sharing knowledge and helping others grow their skills is something I find incredibly rewarding.

Over the years, I’ve cultivated a unique skill set that blends creativity, technical depth, and a genuine passion for crafting engaging digital experiences. I’m always striving to learn, grow, and push the boundaries of what I can build.

Feel free to reach out to me about my skills, prior experience, or to chat about potential collaboration on future projects!
